◆ vertex_road

std::vector<VertexRoad> vertex_road {}

Used to describe a more detailed form of objects inside their bounding box.

They are mutually exclusive with <vertexLocal> elements. <vertexRoad> elements describe a skeleton polyline of objects relative to the road reference line with their s- and t-coordinates.
